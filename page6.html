ABOUT GAME & RULES

In this game, you are given two options, big and small, and you have to choose one. You make this choice based on logic. Once chosen, you can place money on your selected option.

After putting money, if the sum of two numbers is less than 10, it will be called small, and if the sum is 10 or more, it will be called big. If your chosen option matches the result, you win, and if not, you lose. If you win, you get 1.95X of the amount you bet, and if you lose, your bet is deducted.
